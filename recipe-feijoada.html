Feijoada - Black Beans Stew

Ingredients Quantity
Dried black beans 8oz
Bone-in beef short ribs 1 1/2 pounds
Bacon strips cooked 4 or more
Smoked sausage in slices 8oz
Diced onion 1 cup
Salt 3/4 teaspoon
Pepper 3/4 teaspoon
Minced Garlic 2 teaspoon
Bay 1 leaf
Chicken broth 1/2 cup
Beef broth 1/2 cup
Water 1 cup

Directions

Rinse and sort beans; soak according to package directions. Meanwhile, place short ribs and bacon in a 6-qt. slow cooker. Add onion, garlic, bay leaf and seasonings; pour chicken broth, water and beef broth over meat. Cook, covered, on high 2 hours.

Stir in beans and sausage. Cook, covered, on low until meat and beans are tender, 5-6 hours. Remove short ribs. When cool enough to handle, remove meat from bones; discard bones. Shred meat with 2 forks; return to slow cooker. Top servings with orange sections. If desired, serve with hot cooked rice.
